Our Mission

At Gyan Ganga Room Tracker (GGRT), we understand the challenges that students and faculty face in navigating large college campuses. Our mission is to simplify the process of finding classrooms, labs, offices, and other locations, allowing you to focus on your studies and work without the hassle of getting lost.

How to Use?

Select College

Start by choosing your college or university from our list of supported institutions.

Search for Room Number

Enter the room number you're looking for, and our system will search the college's database to find its exact location

Enter Reference

Enter the enterance location from where you wanna navigate the room

Get Room Details

Once the room is found, GGRT will provide you with detailed information, including the department name, floor number, directions from the building entrance, and a reference point picture to help you identify the location easily
