Campus Room Finder was conceived as a college project with a clear vision: to make every corner of our vast campus accessible to everyone. We understand the challenge of locating rooms and facilities in a sprawling educational institution, and we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

connect with us

Our Story

Navigating a college campus can be daunting, especially for those new to the environment. Our team, a group of dedicated students, noticed the need for a reliable guide to help individuals find their way to various rooms across campus buildings. Thus, Campus Room Finder was born.

Our Purpose

Our platform is more than just a digital map; it’s a comprehensive tool designed to assist students, faculty, and visitors in finding their destination quickly and efficiently. Whether you’re searching for a lecture hall, a lab, or a study room, our service is your personal campus concierge.

Our Approach

We believe in simplicity and accuracy. Our user-friendly interface allows you to search for any room within the campus by building, floor, or department. Real-time updates ensure that you are always on the right path, even when room assignments change at the last minute.

Our Promise

We are committed to enhancing your campus experience. Campus Room Finder is constantly evolving, incorporating user feedback to improve functionality and ease of use.
