Welcome to Gyan Ganga Room Tracker

Our room finder tool allows you to quickly locate your classroom by simply entering the room number. Get detailed information about the building, department, floor, direction, and even see a photo of the class before heading there. Simplify your university experience with our room finder.
FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How can I search for a specific room?You can search for a specific room by entering the room number in the search bar on the website.
Can I see photos of the rooms before booking?Yes, you can view photos of the rooms by entering the room number and accessing the photo gallery.
Is it possible to filter rooms based on department?Yes, you can filter rooms based on department by selecting the department name from the dropdown menu.
How accurate is the information provided about the rooms?The information provided about the rooms is regularly updated to ensure accuracy and reliability.
What should I do if I encounter an issue with the room finder website?If you encounter any issues with the website, please contact our support team for assistance.
